# Privacy Policy

**Osho Discourses** does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

## What the app does

- Downloads audio files from oshoworld.com to your device
- Stores your settings, bookmarks, and playback progress locally on your device
- All data stays on your device — nothing is sent to any server

## Data stored on your device

- **Settings** — your preferences (theme, language, playback options) stored in UserDefaults
- **Bookmarks** — timestamps and notes you create, stored as a JSON file in the app's Documents folder
- **Playback position** — which discourse you were listening to and where you stopped
- **Downloaded files** — audio files you choose to download, stored in the Documents folder (visible in Files app)

## Network access

The app connects only to oshoworld.com to stream or download audio discourse files. No analytics, no tracking pixels, no third-party SDKs.

## Third-party services

None. The app uses no third-party libraries, SDKs, or services.

## Analytics and tracking

None. The app does not use any analytics or tracking tools.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, open an issue at https://github.com/aagrawal207/OshoDiscourses/issues
